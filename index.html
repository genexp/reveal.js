How do you lead a great team

If you're not technical, don't fake it

Ask questions, be available for answers.

Measure success two fold: Are you happy, Are we productive?

A great leader can make all the difference

How technical you are directly correlates to how active you are creating & operating the software you're building

Share information freely

Talk a lot, in a way that doesn't impede productivity

Don't cancel projects.

Don't have backdoor meetings

Don't put people on the team who aren't fully invested.

Everyone is a "developer" in that they create products or output. There's no place on the team other than the product owner for people who don't participate in the process. Ideally there's no place for people participating part time in the process.
